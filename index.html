How to play with space

Nebula has two ways to play with a universe. Force modes let your cursor push particles. Space geometry lets you bend the surface they live on — the rubber-sheet idea from general relativity. Turn off the cursor field, drop a well, and watch free particles draw geodesics with their trails.

Mouse / Touch

  • Move — field follows you
  • Click / hold — stronger force
  • Drag — paint velocity into the void
  • Shift+click — place hill (negative well)

Keyboard

  • 1–6 force modes
  • G toggle Force / Geometry layer
  • 7–0 geometry tools
  • M toggle metric grid
  • X clear geometry
  • Space pause
  • R reset · C clear trails
  • N new seed · E export
  • T tone · F fullscreen
  • ? this help · [ ] panels
