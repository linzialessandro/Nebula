How to play with space

Nebula has two simulation modes. Playground is a force + rubber-sheet discovery toy. Relativity integrates Schwarzschild geodesics (single mass) and weak-field multi-mass motion for massive particles and photons — educational GR, not numerical relativity.

Mouse / Touch

  • Move — force field (Play)
  • Click / hold — stronger force
  • G + click — place well / mass
  • Shift+click — hill (Play only)

Keyboard

  • P Playground ↔ Relativity
  • \ focus UI (hide panels)
  • 1–6 force modes (Play)
  • G Force / Geometry layer
  • 7 place well / mass
  • M grid / equipotentials
  • Space pause · R reset
  • F fullscreen · ? help
  • [ ] panels
